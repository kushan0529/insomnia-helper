WHY IN-PERSON MEETUPS

Meetups exist to do three things.

Every group, every session, every circle is designed around these three outcomes. We measure ourselves against them.

🌧️

Defeat Depression

Isolation is the fuel that depression runs on. When you sit with others who are going through the same thing — and no one flinches — depression loses its grip.

Build Real Confidence
